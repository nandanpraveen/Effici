Effici Calendar App is designed to respect your privacy. We do not collect any personal information or data from your device unless explicitly provided by you. The only user data we may collect is:

Effici Calendar App uses the information you provide only for the purpose of enhancing your calendar experience. Specifically:

Your privacy is a top priority, and we take measures to ensure the security of your data:

Effici Calendar App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years old.
